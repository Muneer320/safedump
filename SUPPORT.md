## Community

- **GitHub Stars** ⭐ — If you find Safedump useful, starring the repo helps others discover it
- **Share crash reports** — Safedump reports are safe to share. Attach them to bug reports.
- **Spread the word** — Blog posts, tweets, and conference talks about Safedump are welcome

## Commercial Support

Safedump is a community-maintained open source project. Commercial support is not currently available.
